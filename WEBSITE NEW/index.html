OUR HERITAGE

Born in
Jaipur's Pink City
Written in the Stars

Some things are not chosen — they are qismet. Born from the looms of Sanganer and the ateliers of the old walled city, Qismet was founded on a conviction that the art of Indian men's dressing deserved a custodian who would never compromise.

Every stitch carries the memory of Rajput courts, lake palaces at dusk, and the unapologetic maximalism that has defined Rajasthani identity for centuries.
